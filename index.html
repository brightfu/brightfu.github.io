Research

I am interested in concurrency verification, refinement-based verification, certified compiler and OS kernel verification. Currently I am leading a group working on the CertiuC/OS-II project, whose goal is to apply the refinement-based verification technique to fully verify the commercial real-time embedded kernel uC/OS-II in the proof assistant tool Coq.
